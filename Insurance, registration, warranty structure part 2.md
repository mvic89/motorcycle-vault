# 1️⃣ Insurance – What Should Be Corrected

You wrote:

> “Insurance can be handled both before / after the agreement depending on the bike.”

Here’s the precise version for Sweden:

### ✅ Correct Structure

- Insurance **can be quoted before agreement**
    
- Insurance **can be prepared after agreement**
    
- But traffic insurance must be active before registration**
    

In Sweden:

- Insurance is tied to the VIN
    
- Registration at Swedish Transport Agency requires active traffic insurance
    
- No insurance = no legal registration
    

So the technically correct wording would be:

> Insurance can be quoted before agreement, but must be activated (based on VIN) before registration and delivery.

That removes ambiguity.

Registration should happen only after:

- If payment isn’t received
    

So your structure is right — just make sure insurance activation is explicitly placed before registration.
