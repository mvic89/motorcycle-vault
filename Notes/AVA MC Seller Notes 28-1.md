### Service and repair staff – purchasing of repair & service parts

1. Create an automatic order (scans the system for backorders)
    
2. Create a purchase list / requisition
    
3. Receive products (register them in the system)
    

The automatic order is created to scan the system for all backorders that should be included in the purchase list.

A purchase list / requisition is then created. If they are unsure about which part they need, they do not search directly in the portal by article number. Instead, they use something I believe is called “model identification” (or similar). This shows a visual representation (exploded view diagram) of spare parts and their placement in the motorcycle, along with their article numbers.

The purchase list has a reference number (a new unique reference number is automatically generated each time a purchase list is created), which they use when ordering the items from the portal. All items from the purchase list are then manually entered into the order in the portal.

The portal order has an order number, which they use to verify that the items ordered match what has been delivered.
