Monica has to print out the invoices on paper to keep track on what's been paid for or not. It's hard to keep track of all the paper.

The Service department doesn't look through the order history when they order more goods. But usually only order 1 spare part at the time when it's needed for repair.

When they've created a purchase order and the articles have arrived,  the goods are registered through 'mottagning av varor' / recieval of goods'. Monica types in the purchase number to display the order. She then gets a recieving number (mottagningsnummer). Now she adds which goods that have arrived from the open purchase order.  After that, she controls that the price corresponds with the printed out invoice of hers.

Every year AVA gets sent a pricelist from each brand with the correct price for all the new bikes. Monica has them printed out. 

Every MC from the price list should by default be registered as model products in the system. But since AVA only orders about 2 motorcycles a week, its' not needed. There would be too many model products of vehicles which they don't order. 

The product id of a MC model product changes to a VIN number (ramnummer) when the customer purchases a motorcycle. When the vehicle has been registered, Toni changes the VIN number to a registration number.

When the offer goes to a purchase order, the model product changes so that it gets an unique model product number (model product number + some unique digits). Monica can use it to search up information about the ordered bike.

When Monica registers the incoming products and changes the product id to an article number, it gets quite complicated since she has to do it manually.

It's hard to do inventory if the storage goods aren't properly categorized. Categorization also makes it easier to search for products in the system. (wheels for instance.)

According to Monica: Gunnar normally doesn't order MC's through a purchase order, but through phone / email instead. That way, Monica gets an invoice without a purchase number (name only) which makes it complicated for Monica and the Service personel to find whom the vehicle belongs to in the system. It also makes it harder to change from order number > VIN number > register number.
